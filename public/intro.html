Duke CSA is
a non-political cultural group that provides services to its members, guides new blue devils especially undergraduate international Chinese students in becoming part of the Duke community. CSA also represents the Duke Chinese undergraduate community at Duke, and promotes Chinese and Asian awareness on Duke campus and within the Durham community.

Every year, CSA organizes all kinds of networking, academic, cultural, and sports events. Check out the events section for more details. CSA will provide lots of amazing service opportunities. Join us and register for years of fun and cultural experience!
